INTRODUCTION                                    *Clipboard*

This plugin's main purpose is to copy the contents of a buffer to the system
clipboard.

COMMANDS                                        *Clipboard-commands*

:Clipboard

MAPPINGS                                        *Clipboard-mappings*

There are no mappings registered by the plugin.
You need to map it according to your preference.

ABOUT                                           *Clipboard-about*
